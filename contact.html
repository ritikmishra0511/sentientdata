FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. If yours isn't here, just send me a message!

Are you open to freelance / consulting work?
Yes! I'm open to freelance data science and ML projects. I can help with EDA, model building, NLP tasks, and data pipelines. Reach out with your project scope and timeline.
Can we collaborate on a blog or YouTube video?
Absolutely. I enjoy technical collaborations — co-authoring articles, guest posts, podcast appearances, or YouTube collab videos around Data Science and ML topics. Let's talk!
Are the courses really free?
The core Data Science and starter ML courses are completely free with a free account. Advanced courses (Deep Learning, MLOps, LLMs) will come with a Pro tier. Core content will always remain free.
How long do you take to reply?
I try to respond to all messages within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent matters, tagging me on Twitter/X usually gets a faster response.
Can I use your blog content or code for my projects?
Yes. All blog posts and code samples are free to use for learning and personal projects. For commercial use or redistribution, please mention the original source and reach out first.
Are you looking for internships or jobs?
Currently, yes! I'm actively looking for Data Science and ML internship opportunities. If you're hiring or know of relevant roles, I'd love to hear from you. Please drop me a message via the form.
